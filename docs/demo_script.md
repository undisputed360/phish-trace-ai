# PhishTrace AI — Demo Video Script (3 Minutes)

## Setup Before Recording

- Open Chrome with extension pinned
- Have VS Code or terminal minimized
- Have the web app open at localhost:5173
- Have the email analyzer page ready
- Test URLs ready: http://faceb00k.com/login and https://www.google.com

---

## [0:00-0:30] Problem & Introduction

**Say:**
"Phishing attacks are the number one entry point for cyber breaches.
Every day, thousands of people click malicious links without knowing
what makes them dangerous. Existing tools just say 'safe' or 'dangerous'
— but they never explain WHY.

Today, I'm presenting PhishTrace AI — an intelligent phishing detection
tool that not only catches threats but explains them in plain English
using AI."

---

## [0:30-1:30] Web App Demo

**Show:**

1. Open web app at localhost:5173
2. Paste: http://faceb00k.com/login
3. Click Analyze

**Say:**
"Let me show you how it works. I'll paste a suspicious URL — notice
this looks like Facebook but uses zeros instead of 'o's.

PhishTrace AI immediately flags this as suspicious with a high risk
score. But here's what makes it different: look at the AI explanation.
It tells you exactly WHY this URL is dangerous — it spotted digit
substitution, the lack of HTTPS, and suspicious keywords.

Scroll down to the URL visual highlighter. You can see the suspicious
digits highlighted in yellow, making it obvious even to non-technical users."

**Show:** 4. Paste: https://www.google.com 5. Click Analyze

**Say:**
"Now let's test a legitimate URL. Google.com comes back as safe with
a low risk score. The AI confirms it's legitimate. No false alarms."

---

## [1:30-2:00] Chrome Extension

**Show:**

1. Open a sketchy-looking website (or just go to the phishing URL)
2. Click the PhishTrace extension icon
3. Click "Scan This Page"

**Say:**
"You don't even need to copy-paste URLs. Our Chrome extension scans
the current page with one click. Same AI analysis, right in your browser."

---

## [2:00-2:30] Email Analyzer

**Show:**

1. Click "Email Analyzer" in navigation
2. Click "Load Sample"
3. Click "Analyze Email"

**Say:**
"Phishing doesn't just come through websites — it often arrives via email.
Our Email Analyzer extracts every URL from an email and checks them all.
Here's a sample phishing email — it found two URLs. One is the real Netflix,
the other is a phishing page. The summary tells you immediately."

---

## [2:30-2:45] Threat Feed

**Show:**

1. Click "Threat Feed" in navigation
2. Show reported URLs

**Say:**
"Users can report suspicious URLs to our community threat feed, helping
everyone stay protected. It's a crowd-sourced defense network."

---

## [2:45-3:00] Closing

**Say:**
"PhishTrace AI combines machine learning, large language models, and a
simple interface to make phishing detection accessible to everyone — not
just security experts.

Built for the Hackverse X Cybersecurity & Privacy track. Thank you."

---

## Tips for Recording

- Use OBS Studio (free) or your screen recorder
- Speak clearly and slightly slower than normal
- Zoom in on important parts (browser zoom 125%)
- Show the actual URL bar when demonstrating
- Cut out any loading delays if needed
